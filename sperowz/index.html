Why sperowz

As the name itself suggests, we at Sperowz are committed to provide those wings to the young minds, which enable them to fly higher and higher...where only sky is the limit.

Sperowz preschool inspires its community to actively seek knowledge and understanding, think independently, reason critically, and embrace challenge. As the science has proved, the child's mind is learning at fastest pace in the tender age of 2-5 years. The more opportunities they are exposed to during this period, greater their capacity to learn and discover their unique abilities.

So, we at Sperowz make sure that the child grabs the basic concepts while at Play, through our Unique Curriculum based on International Standards.

Small class sizes promote individualized instruction and allow teachers to use all the facets of environment to stimulate learning by a warm and caring community.
